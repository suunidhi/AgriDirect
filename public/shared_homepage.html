Our Mission & Passion

At AgriDirect, we empower natural farmers by providing them with an independent digital marketplace, free from middlemen. Our QR code verification system ensures transparency, allowing consumers to trace product origins, verify farming methods, and support ethical agriculture. Join us in revolutionizing the way we connect with our food sources.

AgriDirect does not buy or sell crops and is not a broker. Instead, we offer you the ability to effortlessly market your crop via our platform.

How It Works

AgriDirect empowers farmers with "QR-based verification", connects them to consumers, and ensures fair pricing & transparency.

1. Farmer Verification – Farmers register, get verified, and receive a unique QR code & certification.

2. Product Listing – Verified farmers list their natural products on the digital marketplace.

3. Consumer Purchase – Buyers browse, scan QR codes for authenticity, and make secure purchases.

4. Order & Delivery – Farmers process orders and deliver directly to consumers.

5. Impact – Fair pricing for farmers, authentic natural food for consumers, and a transparent supply chain.
