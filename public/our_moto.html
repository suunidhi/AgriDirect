KNOW-YOUR-CUSTOMER

Know-Your-Customer (KYC) is the process we use to verify the identity and origin of finance of users in our digital market place. Thus, preventing any money laundering and the financing of terrorist activities over our platform, whilst creating a network with only trustworthy agricultural buyers and sellers.

KYC FOR SELLERS

Seller accounts are verified by AgriDirect and our partners. They are responsible for reviewing the company's registration document, proof of I.D. and user residence, and proof of company IBAN issued by that seller's bank. The sellers are aunthenticate by sacnner.

KYC FOR BUYERS

Buyer accounts are verified by AgriDirect and our partners. The buyer KYC process is equal to the seller’s, but is not mandatory unless the buyer wants to retract funds from their AgriDirect wallet.The buyers are aunthenticate by sacnner.
